About:

Most students and young adults finish school or college without learning essential real-world skills. Things like paying taxes, performing CPR, fixing a fuse, or basic legal and financial knowledge are completely skipped in traditional education. This leads to gaps that show up during adulthood when these skills are needed most. We plan on teaching you these important skills and spread awareness on them.

FAQ:

Q: What's this app about?
A: Teaches real-life skills like taxes, CPR, home fixes, etc. Stuff school doesn't cover.

Q: Who's it for?
A: Students, young adults, anyone who feels unprepared for adulting.

Q: How does it know what to teach me?
A: You enter age, location, interests, it shows what skills you might be missing.

Q: What kind of skills?
A: Finance, First Aid, Home, Career, Legal, Driving, Tech Safety, Cooking, etc.

Q: Are lessons long?
A: Nope, short modules. Around 15-20 mins max. Super practical.

Q: How's progress tracked?
A: Dashboard shows what's done, what's left, and your category-wise progress.
